Family Physician

Description

Work in settings such as physicians' offices and hospitals where they see patients for acute care and general health maintenance.

Average Salary

$251,333

Employers

  • Clinics
  • Private Practice
  • Hospitals

Related Careers

  • Pediatrician: a doctor who focuses on the health of infants, children, adolescents and young adults
  • General Practicioner: treat common medical conditions and perform routine exams
  • General Surgeon: doctors who specialize in surgical procedures

Detailed Information

Diagnose, treat, and provide preventive care to individuals and families across the lifespan. May refer patients to specialists when needed for further diagnosis or treatment.
