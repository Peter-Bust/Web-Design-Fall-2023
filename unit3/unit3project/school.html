Stress from School and our Social Lives

School causes up to 55% of all stress in teenagers lives. Out of evey activity and obligation we have, school causes over half of stress and anxiety. Majority of this stress is caused by anxiety over tests.

Socialization can also cause a significant amount of stress in our lives. We want to impress and be better than people around us. If we eliminate our extremely high standards and try to better ourselves for ourselves and not others.
